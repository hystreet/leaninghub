Where can I find my Stripe keys?

To find your stripe keys, you’ll need to visit https://dashboard.stripe.com/

After logging in, follow the API link in the left hand column. This page will show you yours publishable (public) and secret live keys. You’ll need to enter these two keys in the branch settings on your staff to take payments.

On the right hand side of the stripe API page you will see a toggle to swap between showing your live and test keys. Simply click this to show your test keys instead.

If you’re unsure of which keys you are currently using, take a look at the fields where you’ve entered the keys on the staff site. Near the start of each key you will see either the words ‘test’ or ‘live’.
