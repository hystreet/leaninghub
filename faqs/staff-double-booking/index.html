Why can I book multiple appointments in the same slot on the staff site?

We have made sure that you, as agents, have complete control over the properties you handle and their attached listings and appointments. Part of this is giving you the ability to book appointments in any time slot, regardless of the status of the slot itself.

Exactly how you use this tool, is entirely up to you. Take a look over here to find out how this would allow you to host open days on properties.
