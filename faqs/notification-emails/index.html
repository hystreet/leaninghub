How will I be notified of developments in the market process?

Right now, You’ll receive email notifications for new bookings, changes in bookings or cancelations made from the consumer site. You’ll also receive notifications surrounding offers from the consumer site.

There’s currently a notification overhaul in the works. As part of this overhaul, you’ll receive email notifications for all changes made on both the staff and consumer site; you’ll never have to worry about being out of the loop on any of the properties you manage.

The overhaul is going to be rolled out in stages across several updates over the next few months- stay tuned!
