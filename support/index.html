Getting support

Hystreet's support team are always ready and eager to answer any questions or queries and help to point you in the right direction so you can get back on track.

Support for the Hystreet system is managed online through your staff portal. This enables you to submit any queries or concerns 24/7 through our support system. When submitting a report please be as thorough as you can – this will help our support team get started with resolving your issue as soon as possible.

If you’ve found something that isn’t working, or if you just want some clarification, hit the tab in the bottom left corner of the staff site. The box that appears in the centre on the page is where you tell us what email address you want us to reply to, what’s gone wrong and how serious the issue is. Once you’ve filled those in, simply hit ‘Send Feedback’.

All of your support queries go straight through to our central support team, where your own dedicated support advisor will manage your query from when it is first submitted, right through to it's resolution, liaising with you every step of the way. As soon as your issue has been resolved, your support advisor will let you know.
