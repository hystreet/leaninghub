Onboarding Checklist

On this page, you'll find a checklist of everything you need to get up and running with Hystreet. Follow this list to make sure that you're all set up to start running real clients through the platform.

  1. Log in
    Once you've received your login details, you'll want to try logging in to the staff site. Have a look around to start familiarising yourself with the platform.
  2. Set your agency up
    Our "Getting Started With Hysteet" guide will help you through these steps.
    • Set up your agency settings
    • Upload your logo and set your colour scheme
    • Add additional branches
    • Setup your agents
    • Setup any other staff logins
  3. Plan a market strategy
    How you chose to market your agency is up to you. Here's a few things you might want to consider:
    • Leaflet
    • Newspaper
    • Google ads
    • Social Media
  4. Check your T&Cs
    Editing your Terms and Conditions is covered in section 2 of our "Getting Started With Hysteet" guide.
  5. Set up your Stripe account
    Our "Setting up Stripe" guide will tell you more about Stripe.
  6. Train your staff
    Feel free to direct all of your agents to the Learning Hub.
  7. Test activities
    here's the main things you'll want to test run:
    • Booking a valuation
    • Entering valuation details & sending valuation report
    • Instructing as a consumer
    • Getting to market
    • Building a listing
    • Approving a listing as the consumer
    • Booking a viewing
    • Entering feedback
  8. Set up for portals
    Our portal guide outlines what you need to know to start publishing to portals.
    • Are you using Hystreet to upload to portals?
    • Have you notified your account manager?
  9. Update your marketing website
    If you haven't set up a marketing site yet, get in touch with our support team. They will contact a web development team for you.
    • put together any new sevice information to be added to your site
    • speak to you web developer about proposed changes
    • identify a live date for your website changes
  10. Introduce your clients to your consumer site
    By this point you'll be quite familiar with the platform. Get in touch with our support team to give us some feedback!
