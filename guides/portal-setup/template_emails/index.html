Template Emails for your account managers

If you are getting set up with the Hystreet platform, you'll need to arrange a new data feed to publish your listings. You can use the email template below to get the conversation with your account manager started:


Hi <account manager name>,

We are currently in the process of setting up an online service to run parallel to <agency>'s traditional service. This online option will be powered by Hystreet, and in order to publish listings generated on this platform, we require a second profile under the <agency>. Please could you confirm the additional monthly cost to have this second profile running along side our existing CRM?

Kind regards,
