Template Emails for your account managers

If you are using the Hystreet platform to handle an online service, you'll need to arrange a new data feed to publish your listings.

you can use the email template below to get the conversation with your account manager started:


Hi <account manager name>,

We are currently in the process of setting up an online service to run parallel to <agency>'s traditional service. This online option will be powered by Hystreet, and in order to publish listings generated on this platform, we require a second profile under the <agency> brand with a branch ID that is specific to the Hystreet network. Please could you provide us with a second branch ID that is part of the Hystreet network and confirm the additional monthly cost to have this second provide along side our existing CRM?

Kind regards,
