Setting Up Your Portals

Hystreet can automatically publish your properties to the portals for you, even updating the listing automatically for you when the status changes (For Sale, Under Offer & SSTC).

Initially you’ll be provided with buttons on the staff interface for making your listing live on the client-facing end of the platform. Once you are comfortable with the process of getting listings to market, get in touch with our support team to start publishing to portals!

We are integrated with the leading portals - Rightmove, Zoopla and OnTheMarket. The process for setting up each is the same though depending on your account manager(s) they may have slightly different terminology for each.

Profiles

Your portal provider will have set you up with a profile for your branch, this may be a profile for Sales, or one for Lettings. If you offer both Sales and Lettings then they will have set you up with a profile for each of those.

The portals can only take one ‘feed’ per profile, this means that you can’t have two CRM providers publishing properties into one profile.

Essentially, you will need to arrange a new feed to publish your listings to the portals.

Getting Started

Once you have decided on the approach you want to take (see examples below or speak to your Hystreet Customer Success Manager) all you need to do is contact your portal account manager(s) and ask them to set up a new profile for you. They’ll be able to advise on their internal process and any information or artwork they will need to get your profile up and running.

you can use this email template to get the conversation with your account managers started:

Hi We are currently in the process of setting up an online service to run parallel to 's traditional service. This online option will be powered by Hystreet, and in order to publish listings generated on this platform, we require a second profile under the brand with a branch ID that is specific to the Hystreet network. Please could you provide us with a second branch ID that is part of the Hystreet network? It is my understanding that because this second profile is on the same brand as our original profile, there will be no additional charge. Please can you confirm that this is the case? Kind regards,

Once they are ready they will contact Hystreet directly and we’ll get the security and feeds configured for you.

As we are dependent on the portals themselves the time for this process can vary but we’ll make sure we get you live as quickly as possible so you can start publishing properties!
