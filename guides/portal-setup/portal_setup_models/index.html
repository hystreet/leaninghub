Portal setup models

There are many possible combinations but weâ€™ve included a few of the most common setups below as a visual guide:

Single branch, one profile.

The most simple setup would be running a single profile (either sales or lettings) for your branch. If you are starting a new business venture to solely offer sales using Hystreet, This will probably your setup.

Single branch, multiple profiles.

If you are already an estabilished agency that offers both lettings and sales using a single CRM, you'll have two profiles (one for lettings and one for sales).

Single branch, multiple profiles, multiple CRMs.

If you use more than one CRM in your branch (i.e. if you use different softwares for your traditional and online services), you may have multiple profiles for sales or lettings. This will be your setup if you are an estabilished agency that offers boths lettings and sales from one CRM, with the addition of a Hystreet platform for your online service.
