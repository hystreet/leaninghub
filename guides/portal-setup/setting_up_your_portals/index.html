Setting Up Your Portals

Hystreet can automatically publish your properties to the Portals for you, even updating the listing automatically for you when the status changes (For Sale, Under Offer & SSTC).

use these buttons to send listings for approval

Initially you’ll be provided with buttons on the staff interface for sending the listing to the seller for approval before it is made live on the client-facing end of the platform. Once you are comfortable with the process of getting listings to market, get in touch with our support team to start publishing to Portals!

We are integrated with the leading Portals - Rightmove, Zoopla and On The Market. The process for setting up each is the same, though depending on the Portal, they might use slight different terminology.

Step 1 - Speak to your account manager
All you need to do to start the setup process is contact your Portal account manager(s) and ask them to set up a new profile for you. They’ll be able to advise on their internal process and request any information or artwork they will need to get your profile up and running.

Step 2 - Let the Hystreet tech team do their thing!

Most of the Portal setup goes on behind in the scenes. Your Account manager will arrange a Branch ID and will usually send it directly to our tech team. If your account manager does give you the branch ID directly, just send it straight over to our support team.

If you have published your listings from another CRM in the past, you'll probably already have a Branch ID saved somewhere. This branch ID won't work with the Hystreet system. You will need to speak to your account manager to get a new profile and branch ID arranged that is set up for your Hystreet platform.

Step 3 - Start publishing to portals

use these buttons to publish your listings to the portals

Once our tech team has entered your branch ID into the system, you will see a new set of buttons appear on the listing presentation page. You'll simply need to hit 'publish' to send your listings off to the portals.
