Profiles

Your portal provider will have set you up with a profile for your branch, this may be a profile for Sales, or one for Lettings. If you offer both Sales and Lettings then they will have set you up with a profile for each of those.

The portals can only take one ‘feed’ per profile, this means that you can’t have two CRM providers publishing properties into one profile.

Essentially, you will need to arrange a new profile to publish your listings to the portals from your Hystreet platform.
