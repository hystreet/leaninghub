Adding a Buyer

Any client wishing to book a viewing or make an offer on a property needs to be added to Hystreet as a buyer. Booking a viewing on the consumer site will automatically add them as a buyer for you.

The buyers page shows a list of all buyers added on both the staff and consumer site.

To add a new buyer, just click the 'new buyer group' button at the top of the page.

Click this button to add new buyers

Buyers are added in groups so that people can buy properties in partnerships. For example, a team of investors may want to buy a property; each investor could have their own log in and any viewings or offers made would be on behalf of every buyer in that group.

On clicking view will allow you to see and edit the name, email address and contact number of each buyer in that group. You can also send password reset emails from this page by clicking the button in the top right.

TIP: Make sure you hit save after making any changes here.
