Main Navigation

On signing in, you’ll be taken straight to the dashboard page. Use the bar at the top of the page to navigate around the site.

Use these buttons to move around the staff site

You'll see buttons to move to the 'Dashboard', 'Properties', 'Buyers', and 'Diary' pages. In the top right corner, you'll see a dropdown menu labelled 'Admin'. Clicking on this will give you buttons to access the 'Settings', 'Branches', 'Agents' and 'Staff' pages. At the bottom of that list, you'll also see the logout button.

Use these buttons to move between the admin sections, or log out

A big chunk of your day-to-day work will be done inside 'properties'. In here, you'll see a list of all properties added to your platform - each with a 'view' button next to them. Clicking 'view' will take you into that property, where you'll see circular buttons for 'property & Sellers', 'Valuation', 'Instruction & Payment', 'Compliance', 'Listing' and 'On The Market'.

Use these buttons to move between the sub-sections on a property

Clicking on these circular buttons will take you into that sub-section. Most subsections are broken down further using tabs - simply click on the tabs to move between the pages of the sub-section.
