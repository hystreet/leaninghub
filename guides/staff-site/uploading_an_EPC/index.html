Uploading An EPC

Before you can upload an EPC (energy performance certificate) for the property, you’ll need to have started building the listing. To add an EPC to the listing, first save the EPC on your computer as you would with any other image, then go to the EPC page of the compliance section. From there, Just click ‘choose file’ and select the image you want to add.

Upload an EPC here

You won’t need to click save on this occasion- this will be saved automatically when the image is added.
