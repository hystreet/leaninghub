Responding to an offer

Once an offer has been submitted, you’ll see it from the property's 'Offers' page. you can access this page by going to Properties > View (on the property) > On The Market > Offers.

You can go back into the offer to update it by clicking view on it. You can update the state of the offer from open to accepted, rejected or withdrawn.

If the buyer wants to withdraw their offer, there’s a field where they can leave a withdrawal statement. Likewise, there’s a field to include a seller’s written response when accepting or rejecting the statement.

Any updates to the offer status will be displayed by showing a ribbon in the top right corner of the featured image on the consumer site.
