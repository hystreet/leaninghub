Updating a buyer's contact preferences

Once you have added a buyer, You'll be able to record their contact preferences by clicking 'view' next to their buyer group's entry on the buyers list, then 'view' again on their name.

Record when the client wishes to be contacted here

After updating your client's contact preferences, be sure to hit 'save user consent' to store the changes.
