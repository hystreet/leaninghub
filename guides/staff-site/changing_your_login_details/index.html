Changing your Login Details

You can change your login details by clicking Admin > Staff > View. You can update your email address by updating the 'Email' field and hitting 'save'. Likewise, you can change your password by typing your new password in the 'Password' field and hitting 'save'.

When you next log into the staff platform, you will need to use these new login details.
