Changing Your Login Details

You can change your login details by clicking Admin > Staff > View. You can update your email address by updating the 'Email' field and hitting 'save'. Likewise, you can change your password by typing your new password in the 'Password' field and hitting 'save'.

be sure to hit 'save' after updating your login details

When you next log into the staff platform, you will need to use these new login details. If you are an Admin user, you'll be able to update any user's login details this way as well.

If you've forgotten your password, you can also reset your password from the 'login' page by clicking 'I've forgotten my password'. This will send you a link to reset your password via email.
