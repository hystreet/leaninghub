Adding Service Features

Once you have sent a valuation report, the next step would be for your clients to instruct and pay you.

As a reminder of the service your clients will be paying for, they will see a list of all of your service features before paying:

Let you're clients know what they are paying for here

This list is fully customisable. You can add and remove points from the 'settings' page of the admin section.

Hit the 'Remove' button to take a feature off the list, or add new features at the bottom of the page.
