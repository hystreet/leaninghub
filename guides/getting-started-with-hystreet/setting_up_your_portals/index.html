Setting up your Portals

We've got quite a lot to tell you about publishing to portals - as such, we've put together a whole guide on the topic of portals. You can find our Portal Guide here.

On the branch settings page, you'll see three fields for Rightmove, Zoopla and OnTheMarket.

These fields are read only - you can see the information, but won't be able to edit it.

You'll see that these fields look slightly different to the other fields on the page, and you won't be able to type anything into them. This is because our tech team will need to update these fields for you behind the scenes.

These fields will show your 'branch IDs' once you have spoken to your portal acount managers and arranged for your portal feeds to be set up.
