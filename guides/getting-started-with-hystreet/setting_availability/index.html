Setting an Agent's availability

we've got a lot to tell you about availability, so we've put together a whole guide on the topics of Availability and Appointments. You can find our Availability and Appointments Guide here.

To make sure your agents are available for bookings, you'll want to keep their availability updated from the availability tab of the 'Agents' section. On this page, you’ll see a grid of checkboxes. Simply click the boxes to identify when you will be available for bookings.

You can check the boxes at the very top of the grid if you want to make yourself available for the entire day.

Click the arrows at the top right corner of the grid to scroll across to later dates.

Once you’ve finished setting your availability, be sure to hit save so you don’t lose what you’ve just entered!
