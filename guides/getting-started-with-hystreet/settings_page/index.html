Accessing The 'Settings' Page

On the staff interface, click on your initials in the top right corner. This will bring up a drop-down list with the buttons to access the 'Settings', 'Branches', 'Agents' and 'Staff' pages.

Click on the 'Settings' button to access the 'Settings' page.

Access the admin pages using these buttons.
