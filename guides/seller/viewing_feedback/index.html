Viewing Feedback

After a viewing, the buyer will be sent reminders to leave feedback on the property.

Feedback is gathered using a star rating system for Interior, Exterior, Location and Overall rating - how buyers leave this feedback is covered in our Buyers' Guide. When a buyer leaves feedback on their property the seller will receive an email notification with a direct link to see the feedback.

Alternatively, sellers can find feedback by going to My account > Manage my sale > See feedback.

This page will show a record of all feedback left on the property. Clicking on the feedback from this page will lead to a detailed view of the feedback, where the seller can see their rating for each aspect and any additional comments left by the buyer.

Feedback helps sellers improve the sellability of their property.
