Booking A Take On

After payment has been received, the next step is to arrange a Take On appointment. The seller can do this by clicking on the 'Complete Checklist' option om the sale overview page, then clicking 'Book Take On'.

Click this to book a take on appointment from the consumer site

This will lead you to a table showing the agent's availability (similar to the table seen when booking a valuation).

The seller just needs to check the time slot that suits them best, then click ‘Book Property Take On’. Just before seeing the confirmation page, they’ll be given some tips on how to best prepare for the take on.

As with booking a valuation, emails to confirm the appointment will be sent out to both the agent and the seller.
