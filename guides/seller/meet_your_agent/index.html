Meet Your Agent

When a seller enters their postcode, the Hystreet system will check the district against your agents' covered districts.

If the entered postcode is covered by an agent, they’ll see a page introducing the agent. Your agent's bio and experience texts will also be displayed to tell your client a little bit about the agent looking after their sale.

This text is fully customisable from the staff site

If the entered postcode district doesn’t have an agent tied to it, the client will still see a message with your branch's contact number to arrange a valuation over the phone (just so you don't lose any potential instructions).
