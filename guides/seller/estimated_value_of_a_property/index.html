The Estimated Value of a Property

Part of the valuation booking process will ask your clients to provide an estimated value for the property.

We've found that this is a really useful piece of information to have. If you've got an idea of the price your clients are hoping to get for their property, you'll be able to tweak your sales pitch on the valuation appointment accordingly.

We've even found that sellers are much more open to giving you this information than if you were to ask the same question in person or over the phone.
