Pre-Valuation Questions

As part of the valuation booking process, the seller will be asked to provide some information about their property to help you be more prepared for the valuation. These questions will give you an idea of the following:

  • Property Type
  • Number of Rooms
  • Parking arrangements
  • Freehold or leasehold status
  • Single or double glazed windows
  • Heating type

The seller will also be asked to provide some basic contact information (just incase you need to get in touch at any point) and a password. From that point on, your client will be able to log back in to their hystreet account using that password and the provided email.

TIP: as part of Hystreet's changes for GDPR, your clients will also be asked to choose when they would like to be contacted. Once logged in, they will also be able to update these preferences by clicking the circle with their initials in the top right corner of the page and selecting My Preferences from the dropdown menu.

To opt-in, your seller will just need to check their prefered options
