Appointment Time Slots

We've got a lot to tell you about how appointments works, so we've put together a whole guide on the topics of Availability and Appointments. You can find our Availability and Appointments Guide here.

When your client gets to the stage of the valuation booking process that asks them to select a time, they will be see a table of all the times the agent the agent is available on. To choose their appointment time, all your seller needs to do is click on the avialable time that works best for them.

This is how the agent's diary will be presented to the seller

When the seller has picked a time, they will be taken to the confirmation page. Both the agent and the seller will receive emails to confirm the new booking.
