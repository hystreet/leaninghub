Updating Seller Availability

If the seller is handling their own viewings or are having accompanied viewings they will need to keep their availability updated. Sellers can choose when they would like to be available for appointments by going to the 'Manage My Availability' section of their account.

This button will only appear once the agent has been instructed

On the 'Manage My Availability' page, the seller can update their availability in the same way an agent would from the staff site; simply by clicking the times they would like to be available. The selected times will then be shown to buyers when they book viewings on that property.

The seller needs to click 'update availability' to save any changes
