The ID Check

Before a property can go on the market, an ID check should be completed on the seller. Hystreet allows your sellers to carry out their own ID checks for you.

To complete an ID check, all the seller needs to do is go to their account, click 'complete checklist' > 'complete ID check'.

At the start of the check, the seller will need to click a box to confirm they are happy for the check to take place.

On the next page, the seller just needs to enter their name, date of birth and home address, then click 'add to ID check'. They will also have the opportunity to add other people involved in the sale.

When the seller clicks 'submit for ID check', the entered information will automatically be sent over to Experian and checked instantly.

The ID check system provides an instant response to your clients

The next screen the seller sees will show them the response to their ID check. In rare instances, Experian might not have enough data on a person to approve their ID. On these occassions you'll need to request a copy of the seller's photo ID and complete an AML check using your traditional method.
