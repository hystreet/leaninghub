Booking A Valuation

Your clients will most likely reach your Hystreet platform by clicking the 'book a valuation' button on your company's website.

TIP: If you still need to set your website buttons up, it might be worth taking a look at our Website Integration guide.

If set up correctly, your web button should lead your clients to a page where they will see a map of your local area of operation and a field to enter their postcode.

Sellers coming from your website will enter their postcode here

If you have directed your clients straight to your hystreet consumer site, they will be able to book a valuation entering their postcode and clicking the button that says: “book your free valuation” in the centre of the page.
