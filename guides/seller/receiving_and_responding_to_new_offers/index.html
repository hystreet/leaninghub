Receiving And Responding To New Offers

After a buyer has viewed a property and left feedback, they may want to submit an offer on the property. We've covered making an offer in our Buyers' Guide.

As soon as this offer is made, both the agent and seller will receive an email notifying them as such. The buyer will also receive an email to confirm the offer has been successfully submitted.

The seller can view this offer by following the link in the email or by going to My account > Manage my sale > See offers.

Any conditions included in buyers' offers will also be visible here

When the seller is ready to respond to the offer, they can accept or reject it by clicking the corresponding buttons. If they would also like to add a message to their response, they can do by clicking the checkbox before clicking a response button.

If the offer is rejected, the buyer will be sent an email notification. They will also be given the opportunity to submit a new offer.

If the offer is accepted, the buyer will be notified, and the seller will see a message like the one below:

The agent will still need to finalise the sale as they usually would

Tip: accepting the offer here is not legally binding. The agent will still need to process this offer as they normally would to finalise the sale.
