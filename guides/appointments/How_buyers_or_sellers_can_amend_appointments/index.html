How buyers or sellers can amend appointments

This is what the 'amend' and 'cancel' appointment links look like

Valuation

If a seller would like to amend the time for a valuation, they can do so by going to account > manage my sale. In the box that says ‘step 1: valuation’, there is a link that says ‘amend appointment’.

Clicking this will take your seller to the time slot table so they can select a new time.

take on

Sellers can also amend their take on appointments by going to account > manage my sale > complete checklist > book property take on (completed). This will take you to the appointment confirmation page.

From here, your seller can click ‘amend appointment’ to see the time slot table and select a new time.

Viewing

Buyers that have confirmed their email address can amend their viewing time by going to account > appointments then clicking on the box for the viewing they would like to amend. This will take them to the booking confirmation page

From here, the buyer can click ‘amend appointment’ to see the time slot table and select a new time.

Please note that sellers can’t amend viewing times but can still cancel them.
