How To View Appointments From The Staff Site

If you're looking for a list of all upcoming appointments, click 'Diary' at the top of the page. This will lead to a page showing table of all appointments scheduled for the future.

From left to right, the table shows: the appointment type ( valuation, take on, or viewing), the property address, start time, end time, and the agent attending the appointment.

You'll also see the option to 'Select Agent' - this will filter the table down to one specific agent's appointments.

click 'view' to see details or amend the appointment

If you're looking for appointments on a specific property, go to that property ('Properties' > 'View') then click 'Appointments'. From here, you'll see an overview of all appointments on that property, including both past and upcoming appointments.
