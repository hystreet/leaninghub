How buyers or sellers can cancel appointments

This is what the 'amend' and 'cancel' appointment links look like

Valuation

If a seller would like to cancel their valuation, they can do so by going to account > manage my sale. In the box that says ‘step 1: valuation’, there is a link that says ‘cancel appointment’. They will be asked to confirm their decision before the cancelation is finalised.

take on

Sellers can also cancel their take on appointments by going to account > manage my sale > complete checklist > book property take on (completed). This will take you to the appointment confirmation page.

From here, your seller can click ‘cancel appointment’. As with valuation cancelations, they will be asked to confirm their decision before the cancelation is finalised.

Viewing

Both buyers and sellers can cancel viewings by to account > appointments then clicking on the box for the viewing. On the appointment confirmation page, they can click ‘cancel appointment’ and confirm their decision.
