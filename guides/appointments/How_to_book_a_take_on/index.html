How to book a take on

Once you have been paid and instructed to sell a property, you will want to book a take on appointment. To do this, go to Properties > View (on the property) > Listing > Book a take on > Add a take on appointment.

Click this to book a take on

Select the agent that is going to carry out the take on, then select the date and time for the appointment.

When selecting a time for the appointment from the staff site, you will be shown which time slots are available or already booked, but you can book the appointment in any slot.

When you've chosen a date and time, click save to book the appointment. At this point, you'll probably want to send a booking confirmation email to the seller by clicking 'Send booked email'.
