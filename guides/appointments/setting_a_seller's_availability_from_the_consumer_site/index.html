Setting a seller's availability from the consumer site

If a seller is managing their own viewings, they will need to set their availability to make sure that buyers are shown time slots to book viewings.

A seller can set their own availability by going to Account > Manage my sale > Manage my Availability. From there, sellers can update their availability in the same way that agents manage their availability from the staff site.

Once they have marked out their desired availability, they can click ‘update availability’ to save their changes.
