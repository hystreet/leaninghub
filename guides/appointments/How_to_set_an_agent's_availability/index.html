How to set an agent's availability

As an agent, it’s a good idea to update your availability regularly to make sure that your clients can book appointments from the consumer site. If you haven’t set your availability for a day, your clients won’t be able to book appointments.

To set your availability, go to the staff site and click Admin > Agents > View (on your name) > Availability. From there, you’ll see a grid of checkboxes. Simply click the boxes to identify when you will be available for bookings.

You can check the boxes at the very top of the grid if you want to make yourself available for the entire day.

Click the arrows at the top right corner of the grid to scroll across to later dates.

Once you’ve finished setting your availability, be sure to hit save so you don’t lose what you’ve just entered!
