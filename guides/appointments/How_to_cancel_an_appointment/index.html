How to cancel an appointment

To cancel an appointment from the staff site, go the relevant appointment type page (see the individual guides). On those pages, you'll see a list of appointments booked on the property. Click 'View' on an appointment to see it in more detail.

On clicking cancel, a confirmation box will pop up to make sure that you are cancelling the appointment intentionally.

Click ‘cancel appointment’ to cancel the appointment. Otherwise, click ‘close’ to go back to the appointment view screen.
