How sellers book a launch

After instructing an agent to sell their property, the next step for a seller is to book a launch. They can do this be going to account > manage my sale > complete checklist > book launch.

The seller will be presented with a table of all available time slots to pick their launch time from. Once they have selected a time they will need to click ‘book property launch’.

They will then be taken to a page that advises them on preparing for the launch appointment (i.e. tidying up and clearing clutter).

Clicking ‘okay- got it’ will take them to the appointment confirmation page.
