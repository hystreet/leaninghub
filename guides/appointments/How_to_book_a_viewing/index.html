How to book a viewing

To book a viewing on a property that is on the market, go to properties > view (on the property) > appointments > viewing > add viewing.

Click this to book a viewing

Select the agent that is overseeing the viewing and the buyer that is viewing the property, then select the date and time for the appointment.

When selecting a time for the appointment from the staff site, you will be shown which time slots are available or already booked, but you can book the appointment in any slot.
