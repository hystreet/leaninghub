How to amend an appointment

Changing the appointment

To amend an appointment from the staff site, go to properties > view (on the property) > appointments.

On this page, you'll see a list of all appointments booked on the property. Click 'view' on an appointment to see it in more detail.

This page will show a list of all appointment types for that property

From there, you can select a new time and date for the appointment by clicking on the fields and selecting a new time. Be sure to hit save, else the updates to the appointment won't be recorded.

Sending update emails

Once you've updated the appointment on your system, you might want to send your clients an updated appointment confirmation email.

For valuation or take on appointments, go into the appointment and click 'Send amended email'.

For viewing apppointments, go into the appointment and click 'details' > Send summary.
