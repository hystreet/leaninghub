How to amend an appointment

To amend an appointment from the staff site, go to properties > view (on the property) > appointments > view (on the appoint you want to update).

How sellers book a valuation

Enter a postcode and click here to book a valuation

From there, click on the fields to update them as desired then click save.

At this point, you might want to send your clients an updated appointment confirmation email by clicking ‘notify participants’.
