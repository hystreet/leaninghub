How buyers book viewings

Your clients can book viewings by going to search properties and clicking on the property they are interested in. This will take them to the listing page for that property. To book the viewing, they will need to click ‘book a viewing’.

The colour of this button will depend on your colour scheme

If they have not yet created an account (or aren’t logged in). They will be asked to create an account or log in.

If your client has already logged in, they will be asked to provide some information on their current position and how they would fund the purchase of the property.

Once this information has been entered, they will be presented with a table of all available time slots to pick their viewing time from. Once they have selected a time they will need to click ‘book a viewing’.

Click on the desired timeslot then hit 'book a viewing'

If your client has already confirmed their account, they will be taken to the appointment confirmation page.

If their account is not yet confirmed, they will be told that their appointment is only provisionally booked until they follow the confirmation link sent to them via email.
