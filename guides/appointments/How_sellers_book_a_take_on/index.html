How sellers book a take on

After instructing an agent to sell their property, the next step for a seller is to book a take on. They can do this by going to account > manage my sale > complete checklist > book take on.

Click this to book a take on appointment from the consumer site

The seller will be presented with a table of all available time slots to pick their take on time from. Once they have selected a time they will need to click ‘book property take on’.

They will then be taken to a page that advises them on preparing for the take on appointment (i.e. tidying up and clearing clutter).

Clicking ‘okay- got it’ will take them to the appointment confirmation page.

These tips should ensure that your take on appointments are as productive as possible
