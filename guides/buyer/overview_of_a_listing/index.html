Browsing Listings

After clicking on ‘Search Properties’ on the homepage, buyers will see a list of all the properties you currently have on the market. For each listing, they’ll see the featured image, the price, the address and counters for bedrooms and bathrooms.

This is how a listing shows up on the consumer site

Listing overview

Clicking on the property they are interested in will bring the buyer to the full property listing page.

Here’s what theyll find on this page:

This is what you'll find inside a property listing

  1. Clicking the logo will take you straight back to the consumer site homepage
  2. This button will take you back to the property listings
  3. This will direct you to your own account, where you manage appointments, feedback and the sale of your own property. We have a seperate guide for using the consumer site as a seller
  4. Clicking this icon will bring up a dropdown menu with quick links to your availability and as well as the option to log out
  5. This is the price of the property. Just above the value, you’ll see the price qualifier
  6. This shows the type and address of the property
  7. Click this to show the property’s location on a map
  8. Here, in the centre of the page you’ll see the property photos. On loading the page, this will show the featured image (usually the front of the property)
  9. This switch allows you to toggle between viewing the map location and the property photos
  10. These arrows allow you to move through all the property photos
  11. This counter shows you the total number of photos on this listing (excluding the EPC and the floorplans) and the number photo you’re viewing right now
  12. Clicking the thumbnails across the bottom of the page will bring up the full-size photo in the centre of the page
  13. Follow this link to arrange a viewing. This page will go into booking viewings in more detail
  14. This button will bring you back to the photo gallery if you are viewing the property’s floorplans or EPC
  15. This button will take you to view the property’s floorplans
  16. This button will take you to view the property’s EPC
  17. This section provides you with some information on the property’s key features
  18. This section shows the main property description of the listed property
  19. These Tabs show a summary of each room in the property. Clicking on them will bring up the photos of that room
