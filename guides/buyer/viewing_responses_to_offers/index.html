Viewing responses to offers

TIP: This guide covers the buyers perspective. If you'd like to find out more about offers from a seller's perspective, try our seller guide.

Once a seller has responded to an offer, they buyer will receive an email to let them know. This email is just a notification, so they will need to log into their Hystreet account to see the response. They can find the responses by going to account > 'properties you're interested in' > offer negotiation.

From here they will a log of all their offers on that property. If the offer is rejected, the buyer will be asked if they would like to submit a new offer. If the offer is accepted, they will be notified as such.
