Amending or cancelling a viewing

Once a buyer has booked a viewing on a property, they will be able to review the appointment by going to account > appointments then clicking on the viewing. This will take them to the booking confirmation page.

At the bottom of the booking confirmation, they will see two links: Amend appointment and Cancel appointment.

If the buyer amends their appointment, they will see the appointment timetable, where they will be able to pick a new appointment time. If the buyer wishes to cancel their appointment, they will need to confirm they understand it may be difficult to re-book before the cancelation is finalised.

buyers will need to check the box to confirm before they can cancel the appointment
